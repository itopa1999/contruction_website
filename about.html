Our Story

Over the years, the Federal Government of Nigeria has faced the challenge of finding diligent indigenous construction companies capable of delivering projects that rival international standards. In response to this, Bldr. Adegite John Adewale founded BENTUNS CONSTRUCTION NIG. LTD in 2010 — a company built on excellence, integrity, and innovation in the construction industry.

The company was incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 1990, and since then has focused on delivering value-for-money projects in building, civil engineering, and telecommunication infrastructure across Nigeria.

  • Incorporated in 2010 by the Corporate Affairs Commission
  • Registered with CORBON & Nigeria Institute of Building
  • Committed to quality, efficiency, and national growth

Our mission is to deliver reliable, durable, and aesthetic structures while upholding the highest standards of professionalism and craftsmanship.
